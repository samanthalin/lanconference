Mission

I know WHERE I can meet WITH my business partner or team.
- In Berlin, Lan Conference is committed to maintain the professionalization of the commercial or academic activity in a pleasant atmosphere.

Lan Conference is to provide shared or own room space for the company, organization or freelancer who locate in Berlin or travel to Berlin. The highlight is cozy and comfortable room space, modern and professional facility and tailor made service.

Vision

I know THERE I can meet my professional partner or team,
to set up routine professional gathering in business and academic field.

Lan Conference is not only a room space, but also the Conference itself.
